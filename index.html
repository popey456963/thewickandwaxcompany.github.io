About Us

The Wick and Wax Company have been making candles in Woodbridge, Suffolk since September 2015. We are passionate about creating unique candles which not only smell great, but don’t harm the environment at the same time. All candles are lovingly hand poured by us. You might spot us out and about at markets around the Woodbridge area.

Our Products

The candles we produce are made using soy wax, which burns cleanly and evenly for longer than the more traditional paraffin wax. Soy wax does not release carcinogens into the air when burnt, unlike paraffin, and also releases a stronger scent; we feel it makes an overall superior candle. We use only natural scents, and no dyes or colouring go into the wax. Currently, you can purchase Apple and Cinnamon, Cool Citrus Basil, Mandarin and Mimosa, Rose and Woodland Walk scents.

Hand-made with Care

Each scent can be purchased in three sizes: small (£6), medium (£8), or large (£10). We update these scents fairly regularly, so check back soon to see what’s new.
